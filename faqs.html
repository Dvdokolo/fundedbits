    General Questions

    4 Topics

    What is FundedBits?

    FundedBits is a proprietary trading firm that provides capital to skilled traders, allowing them to trade forex, crypto, and other markets without risking personal funds.

    How does the FundedBits funding process work?

    Traders undergo a two-step evaluation process to qualify for a funded account. Upon passing, they can trade with the firm's capital and keep a percentage of the profits.

    What markets can I trade with FundedBits?

    You can trade in forex, crypto, commodities, indices, and more through our advanced trading platforms.

    How do I get started?

    To get started, simply sign up on our platform, select an evaluation model, and begin trading to meet the required profit targets.

    Evaluation & Funding Process

    5 Topics

    What is the Evaluation phase?

    The Evaluation phase is a challenge to assess your trading skills. Traders must meet a profit target within set rules to progress to a funded account.

    Is there a minimum number of trading days?

    No, you can complete the challenge at your own pace as long as you meet the profit target within the time limit.

    What is the profit target?

    The profit target varies by account type but typically ranges between 8-10%.

    Can I reset my evaluation if I fail?

    Yes, we offer an account reset option where you can restart the evaluation for a fee.

    What happens if I breach the rules during evaluation?

    A breach in any rule (e.g., exceeding maximum drawdown) will result in immediate disqualification from the evaluation phase.

    Trading Rules

    4 Topics

    What is the maximum daily drawdown allowed?

    The maximum daily drawdown is 5%, which must be adhered to at all times.

    Can I trade on news releases?

    Yes, you are allowed to trade during high-impact news releases, but ensure you manage your risk appropriately.

    Are there any restrictions on holding trades overnight or over the weekend?

    No, FundedBits does not impose restrictions on holding trades overnight or over weekends.

    What trading platforms do you support?

    We support Matchtrader and TradingView providing flexibility for all trading styles.

    Are there any withdrawal fees?

    No, FundedBits does not charge any fees for withdrawals.
