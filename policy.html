    FundedBits Privacy Policy


    Information Collection:

    We collect personal information such as name, email, and financial details when you sign up, use our services, or interact with our platform. We may collect additional information to improve your user experience, such as IP addresses, device types, and browsing patterns.

    Data Usage:

    The collected data helps us provide better services, personalize your experience, process transactions, and ensure the security of our platform. We do not share your information with unauthorized third parties.

    Cookies:

    We use cookies to enhance the functionality of our website and gather information about how users interact with our platform. Users can manage cookie preferences via browser settings.

    Third-Party Services:

    We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform, process payments, and provide customer support. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.

    Data Security:

    FundedBits employs encryption and secure storage to protect your personal information from unauthorized access. We routinely update our systems to comply with evolving security standards.

    User Rights:

    You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request information about how we process your data and object to certain processing activities, such as marketing communications.

    Changes to the Privacy Policy:

    We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be communicated through our platform, and continued use of our services implies acceptance of the updated terms.

    Contact Us:

    For questions about your personal data or this policy, please contact us at hey@fundedbits.com
